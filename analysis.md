# 📊 Sales Analysis Report (2015–2018)

---

## 🎯 What Was the Goal?
Find out:
- Is the business growing?
- Which region sells the most?
- Which products are making money?
- Which products are losing money?

---

## 📈 Step 1: Year-wise Sales

| Year | Orders | Total Sales (₹) | Growth |
|------|--------|-----------------|--------|
| 2015 | 1,953 | 4,79,856 | — |
| 2016 | 2,055 | 4,59,436 | **-4.26%** ⚠️ |
| 2017 | 2,534 | 6,00,192 | **+30.64%** ✅ |
| 2018 | 3,258 | 7,22,052 | **+20.30%** ✅ |

**Key Points:**
- 2016 was a bad year — sales went down even though orders went up
- 2017 was a strong comeback — sales jumped by 30%
- 2018 continued growing — best year overall
- In 2 years, business grew by **57%** (₹4.6L → ₹7.2L)

---

## 🌍 Step 2: Which Region Performs Best?

| Region | Sales 2016 | Sales 2017 | Sales 2018 | Consistent? |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **West** | 1,33,710 | 1,82,471 | **2,48,131** | ✅ Yes |
| East | 1,53,225 | 1,78,512 | 2,10,129 | Slow growth |
| Central | 1,02,425 | 1,45,674 | 1,41,627 | ❌ Declined |
| South | 70,076 | 93,536 | 1,22,165 | Steady |

**Key Points:**
- **West region is the winner** — grew 36% both years, very consistent
- East was biggest in 2016 but grew slowly, West overtook it by 2018
- Central grew fast in 2017 (+42%) but then fell in 2018 (-2.78%) — not reliable
- South is steady but small

> ✅ **West region selected for deeper analysis**

---

## 🏷️ Step 3: Which Category Sells Best in West?

| Category | Sales 2016 | Sales 2017 | Sales 2018 | Trend |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| **Technology** | 45,098 | 62,099 | **95,477** | ✅ Growing fast |
| Office Supplies | 37,421 | 47,131 | **81,625** | ✅ Growing fast |
| Furniture | 51,191 | 73,241 | 71,029 | ❌ Declined in 2018 |

**Key Points:**
- **Technology** nearly doubled in 2 years (+111%) — biggest growth
- **Office Supplies** had a slow start but exploded in 2018
- **Furniture** was the top category but went negative in 2018 — losing ground
- Focus should be on Technology and Office Supplies going forward

- **Storage and Appliances** struggled in 2017 but recovered well in 2018

---

## 📊 Step 5: Which Sub-Category Contributes Most to Growth?

| Sub-Category | Contribution 2016-17 | Contribution 2017-18 |
|--------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| **Copiers** | -3.78% | **33.83%** 🥇 |
| **Binders** | 31.24% 🥇 | **21.06%** 🥈 |
| **Storage** | -10.63% | **17.65%** 🥉 |
| Accessories | 17.86% | 15.26% |
| Appliances | -10.2% | 12.47% |
| Phones | 25.53% | **-1.36%** ❌ |
| Supplies | 22.95% | **-6.48%** ❌ |

**Key Points:**
- Just **3 products (Copiers, Binders, Storage)** are driving **73% of total growth**
- This is the classic **80/20 rule** — few products, big impact
- Copiers went from worst (-3.78%) to **best (33.83%)** — biggest turnaround in the data
- Phones went from top performer (25.53%) to **negative (-1.36%)** — market is saturating
- Supplies are actively hurting growth (-6.48%) — needs urgent fix

---

## 🚀 Final Recommendations

### ✅ What to Focus On:

1. **Invest more in Copiers**
   - Grew 4x in 2 years, now the #1 growth driver
   - Find out what caused this spike and repeat it in other regions

2. **Keep growing Binders and Storage**
   - Both are consistent and reliable performers
   - Increase stock, introduce new variants

3. **Push Technology category hard**
   - Sales doubled in 2 years (₹45K → ₹95K)
   - Target offices and businesses who buy in bulk

4. **Copy West region strategy in South region**
   - West has 35%+ consistent growth every year
   - South is also growing well — good place to test the same approach

---

### ⚠️ What to Fix:

5. **Phones are slowing down**
   - Once the top product, now declining
   - Solution: focus on bulk or enterprise sales, or bundle with accessories

6. **Supplies are dragging growth down (-6.48%)**
   - Biggest problem product right now
   - Check if competition or pricing is the root cause

7. **Furniture category needs a new strategy**
   - Was the top category, now went negative in 2018
   - Target new customers — new office setups, renovations, commercial buyers

8. **Investigate Central region before investing more**
   - Grew 42% in 2017, then fell -2.78% in 2018
   - Understand *why* it declined before spending more money there

---

## 📌 Final Summary

> **Business grew 57% in 2 years — from ₹4.6L to ₹7.2L**

> **West region + Technology + Copiers = the 3 main growth drivers**

> **Phones, Supplies, and Furniture are declining — need urgent attention**
